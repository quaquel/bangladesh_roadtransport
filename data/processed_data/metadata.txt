Processed data used for further analysis.

1. District_level_data_v6
Key statistics on district level for model inputs

2. _roads3.csv
Location Reference Points of all roads in Bangladesh with corrected longitude-latitude information

3. road_rmms_v9.shp
Shapefile of fully connected and routable road network from the RMMS database.

4. BGD_Districtdata_Citycentroid_level2_v02.shp
Shapefile of centroids of each district. Centroid represents the biggest city in the district.
